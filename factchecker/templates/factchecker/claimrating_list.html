About us

This project is an experiment by the Futures Lab of the Reynolds Journalism Institute. Our aim is to distill the editorial process and content format of fact-checking publications, such as PolitiFact, and demonstrate how other news organizations could replicate these tools.

We're focusing on newsworthy claims, by newsworthy people and organizations, related to education in Missouri. If you've got a claim you would like for us to check out, please pass it along.
